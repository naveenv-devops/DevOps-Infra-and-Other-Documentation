# Quick start

Get your first document conversion running on Met-R in under 15 minutes.

---

## Prerequisites

- Met-R workspace account with **Author** role or above
- Supported browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox — latest 2 versions)
- Network access to `*.met-r.io`
- Source file in a supported format
- Active subscription (see [Licensing](../reference/licensing.md))

Full checklist: [Prerequisites](prerequisites.md)

---

## Step 1 — Sign in

1. Go to [met-r.io](https://met-r.io) (or your organization's Met-R URL)
2. Sign in with your workspace credentials
3. Open the **Dashboard**

---

## Step 2 — Upload source content

1. Navigate to **Documents** → **Upload**
2. Select your source file
3. Choose the **source format** (e.g. FrameMaker, DITA, Markdown)
4. Click **Upload**

---

## Step 3 — Run a conversion

1. Open the uploaded document
2. Click **Convert**
3. Select target format (e.g. DITA, HTML5, PDF)
4. Choose a conversion profile (or use the default pipeline)
5. Click **Run conversion**

Met-R queues the job and runs it through the appropriate converter service.

---

## Step 4 — Review output

1. When status shows **Complete**, open the **Output** tab
2. Preview HTML or download PDF/XML
3. Review validation results (schema check, XSLT validation)

---

## Step 5 — Publish (optional)

1. Click **Publish**
2. Select target channel: Infinity DocSite, CMS, or file download
3. Confirm publication
