# DOCX converter

Convert Microsoft Word documents to DITA and HTML.

**Repository:** `metR-Converter-DOCX`  
**Status:** GA

---

## Overview

The DOCX converter migrates Word `.docx` files into structured topics while preserving headings, lists, tables, and cross-references where possible.

---

## Supported inputs

| Input | Extensions |
|---|---|
| Microsoft Word | `.docx` |
