# Contact support

---

## MetaPercept support channels

| Channel | Use for |
|---|---|
| **Email** | support@metapercept.com |
| **Account manager** | Billing, licensing, new workspaces |
| **Enterprise support** | Platform incidents (Enterprise plan customers) |

---

## Before contacting support

1. Note your workspace name and user email
2. Capture the job ID or error message
3. Check [Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md) and [FAQ](../reference/faq.md)

---

## Severity levels

| Level | Description | Response target |
|---|---|---|
| **P1 — Critical** | Platform down, data loss risk | 1 hour |
| **P2 — High** | Major feature unavailable | 4 hours |
| **P3 — Medium** | Degraded performance, workaround exists | 1 business day |
| **P4 — Low** | Questions, minor issues | 2 business days |
